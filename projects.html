Replacing a "non-removable" mobile phone battery

This isn't the prettiest thing I've coded, but the intent wasn't to show how pretty I can code. The intent was to show how I solved a real-world problem. My spouse was having issues with a cell phone. The battery was showing signs of dying. It had moments of rapidly losing a charge. Once I ruled out other possible issues as the reason for the drain, I deduced it was the battery. In this, I solve the issue of replacing a non-removable battery in a mobile device. This is my work on an LG K51 where the "non-removable" battery is hooked up to the motherboard. I replaced the battery. 1.5 months later, the new battery is holding the charge like a champ. December 2023.
