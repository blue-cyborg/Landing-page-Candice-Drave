Hi. I'm Candice

Front-end Developer and Technology and Innovation Management Ph.D Student from New York

Hi, I'm Candice. I like coding. I just like doing things with computers and technology in general. I've had a varied educational background. I started in the arts and made a master's program and thesis focused on the history of technology and how it has shaped culture. When I graduated from that program, I decided to chase my true love of tech. I am currently a Ph.D. student in a technology and innovation management program. I've made a share of assignments about building inclusive projects and LGBTQ+ projects. Building inclusive technology projects and LGBTQ+ projects are something I care deeply about. All tech should be inclusive, and also all LGBTQ+ people should have resources at their fingertips and be thought of when making policies and strategies. Growing up queer shaped this part of me. Since my teen years, I have wanted to learn to code. Over this past year, I became serious about it and am working through all the SheCodes programs and certificates.

Outside of tech, I love taking care of my beta fish and turtle family. I love my partner of 13 years, Tom. I am really into collecting old video games and playing them on my Retron 5. I'm a huge nerd about pizza. Any house I live in has to have a pizza stone and electric oven that I can make super hot. I love crafting my own dough, sauces, and cheese blends. I want to make an original great NY style pizza.
